Welcome to The Anti Net Work Hub

I hope you enjoy your stay with me!

The Anti Network Idea is a few years old, and has become something of an evolving internet art project, where the founder, I, JD, can speak into the void and see if the void speaks back.

I have strived to give open communication and sharing of art, the good, the bad, and the ugly, for my peers and my self.

As of now, the Anti Network, offers a Basic BBS for Sharing of writing of prose and poetry, including prompts weekly and monthly, a photo sharing server for peers, as well as a semi-used discord.

The possibilities are as endless as the free server space I can milk out of these hosting companies. I plan on doing as much as I can for as long as I can, and really feed this Tulpa.

I am going to be adding a shop for my 3D prints in the near future as well, and offering sound design services, BG Music.
