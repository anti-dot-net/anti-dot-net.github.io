Welcome to The Anti Net Work Hub

I hope you enjoy your stay with me!

The Anti Network Idea is a few years old, and has become something of an evolving internet art project where I can speak into the void and see if the void speaks back.

I have strived to give open communication and sharing of art, the good, the bad, and the ugly, for my peers and my self.

I plan on doing as much as I can for as long as I can, and really feed this Tulpa.

Very soon will have a shop for random things like poetry books, 3d and ink block prints, care packages, maybe even mixtapes (again...)
